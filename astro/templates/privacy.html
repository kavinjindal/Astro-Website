Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy for Astro


What Data Do we store?

-> User IDs
-> Guild IDs
-> Guild Invite Links
-> Channel IDs

Why do we store the data?

-> We store the user IDs to manage our economy system. All the currency of our economy system is saved using the user IDs.It makes it easy for us to manage the data.
We store the server IDs to keep a track of the servers in which our bot is working. We have a database which is manually edited, to keep a record of the servers where our bot is present.

->We store the channel IDs to show our statistics and keep a record of the text channels where our bot is used.

->We store the invite links of the servers (generated by our bot) for tracking and management. We have an advanced error logging system which will notify us about any error that is caused by our bot during the usage of the command. If its a code error, then it will automatically report us on our support server. And it will also generate a server link so that we can enter the server and inspect the problem.


Do we share your data with anyone?

No, user data is not shared with any third party service, it is only accessible to the developers of the bot.

How to contact us for queries or data removal?

You can either join our Support Server or email us on kavinsjindal@gmail.com


How do I get my data removed?

To get your data removed, you can simply remove the bot from your server to prevent the bot from storing your guild information. Or else you can email us to not store your guild data.If you don't want us to store your Profile Information, you can contact us on our email or reach out to us on our support server. If you want to get your user info removed and not to be stored in future, then it will result in blacklisting you from using our currency feature as its necessary to store the user info to manage the economy system.
